# Keywords and Precedence

## Hard Keywords

`let`, `var`, `fn`, `if`, `else`, `for`, `in`, `to`, `while`, `repeat`,
`match`, `try`, `use`, `return`, `say`, `ask`, `spawn`, `do`, `and`, `or`,
`not`, `true`, `false`, `of`, `is`, and `public`.

## Soft Keywords

`record`, `choice`, `shape`, `test`, `times`, `together`, and `where`.

## Precedence

1. postfix: calls, methods, fields, indexes
2. unary minus
3. multiplication, division, modulo
4. addition, subtraction
5. range `to`
6. comparisons
7. `not`
8. `and`
9. `or`
